Mystery McCulloughs

Most of these people are almost definitely McCullough family members, descendants of Sheridan McCullough. A few  are descendants of Jacob Broom Lyon & Nancy Jane Ross. Pictures taken between 1855 and 1890. Click on the photos to enlarge them. Recognize anyone? E-mail us .

I also have many identified pictures from this same time period (1855 - 1900) for Sheridan and William (b. 1828) McCullough's descendants, which are not on the site. Also some Lyon family, and the Thomas-Hays-Dufford branches. If you are looking for any of these particular people, let me know.
