Generation No. 1

1. William Sheridan McCullough, born 28 January 1863 in Mechanicsville, PA; died 5 October 1941 in Pittsburgh, PA. He was the son of 2. William McCullough and 3. Elizabeth Jane Frampton. She was the daughter of Jacob Broom Lyon and Nancy Jane Ross.
